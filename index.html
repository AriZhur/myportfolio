About me

As a first-year New Media Arts student at the Polish-Japanese Academy of Information Technology, I am interested in Graphic Design and media. Working on my bachelor's degree and have been taking every available opportunity to gain practical experience in the field.

Currently, I focus on creating posters, symbols, logos and brand identities while staying up-to-date with emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence and its capabilities. I typically use Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, Figma and Procreate.

My work

I prefer to construct my own knowledge and understanding of the world, rather than simply receiving it.
From my childhood I observe, practice, and love to collaborate.

So my approach to work is no different.

It takes time for me to do a research, gather some information, and critically analyse the task. I always ask myself the question "Does this make sense here?" to ensure that I am on the right track and my work is aligned with the goals of the project.

I also maintain a rationally positive attitude towards life, which helps me navigate through challenges with a clear mind and focused approach. In stressful situations, I rely on my critical thinking skills to make informed decisions and find creative solutions.
